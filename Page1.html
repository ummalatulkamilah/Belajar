Tech time not to blame for teens' mental health problems

Study of 400 teens finds little evidence linking excessive smartphone use and
mental health outcomes

A new study suggests that the time adolescents are spending on their phones and online is not that bad.
The study tracked young adolescents on their smartphones to test whether more time spent using digital technology
was linked to worse mental health outcomes.
